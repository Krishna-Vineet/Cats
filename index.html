Russian

Pink Cat

Russian Blue cats are known for their elegant, short-haired red-pink coat and striking clean eyes. They are gentle, smooth, and often form strong bonds with their owners.

Indian

Black Cat

The Indian Domestic Cat is a common breed with a dark appearance. They adapt well to various positions and are known for their playful nature.

Thailand's

Big Cat

The Siamese cat, originating from Thailand, is recognized for its sleek body, short coat, and striking big melon-shaped eyes. Thailand cats are vocal, social, and enjoy human interaction.

Japanese

Blur Cat

The Japanese Blurtail is a unique breed with a short, blurred back. Known for their playful and friendly demeanor, they have a distinctive voice. In Japan, they are the symbol of fun.
